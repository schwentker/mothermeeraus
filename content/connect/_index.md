This section gathers practical ways to remain connected to Mother Meera’s work in the United States. It brings together local groups, volunteering, updates, and donation information in one place.

## Volunteer

If you are interested in service, there are many different ways to support Mother’s activities. The opportunities range from extended stays in India, to organizing meditations in the US, to creating videos for the Foundation’s YouTube channel. The level of involvement depends on your availability, life experiences, job skills, and resources. Whatever you have to offer, there is likely a volunteer opportunity for you.

When Mother Meera visits the US, volunteers are needed at each tour location. From pre-tour preparations, to event set up, to volunteering in the bookstore or the meditation hall, there are many opportunities to join the team and support Mother’s work with your service.

Volunteers are also needed at the Mother Meera schools in India. Mother’s vision is to give Indian children a high standard of education in schools, and western volunteers are welcome to support that effort. Help with construction is also highly valued.

Please note that Mother Meera Foundation USA does not sponsor volunteer opportunities outside the United States, and we list them here as a service to those who would like to support Mother’s activities abroad.

## Stay Connected

In addition to volunteering, donating, and connecting with [local groups](/connect/local-groups/), there are other ways to stay connected.

### Receive Emails

To receive emails with announcements about upcoming darshan tours, tour updates, and Mother Meera Foundation USA activities, please [subscribe to the email list](https://registration.mothermeera.com).

### Receive Texts

Another way to stay connected to Mother Meera throughout the year is to sign up for text notifications from Mother Meera Foundation USA. You can receive texts about USA darshan tours, and alerts about Mother’s whereabouts and work, updates on her school in India, recent photos, and new items in the bookstore.

To sign up for USA darshan tour announcements only, text `MMTOURS` to `877-566-3372`.

To sign up for USA darshan tour announcements, alerts, updates, and photos, text `MEERA` to `877-566-3372`.

Message and data rates may apply. The former site stated that no more than 4 texts per month would be sent, and that you could opt out at any time by texting `STOP`.

Other ways to stay connected are to receive Mother’s blessings via [YouTube](https://www.youtube.com) and read the posts on the [blog](/blog/).

## Donations

Your donation to Mother Meera Foundation USA supports Mother Meera’s work in four main ways:

1. It helps fund darshan tours when Mother visits the US.
2. It helps maintain the Foundation home in Boone, North Carolina. It is possible that in the future this home could have additional public uses beyond local meditations, including use as a darshan hall.
3. It helps develop and distribute bookstore items through the [bookstore](https://mother-meera-bookstore-usa.myshopify.com). These items, such as photos of Mother, books about her, japamalas, incense, and so on, can serve to bring people the experience of Mother’s presence and grace.
4. It helps support Mother Meera’s schools and almost 2,000 students aged 3-16 in Madanapalle, India.

Please note that since Mother Meera Foundation USA is a 501(c)(3) nonprofit organization, all donations are tax deductible in the United States. You will receive a receipt for your donation for tax purposes. Mother Meera and Mother Meera Foundation USA reserve the right to determine how to use all donated funds.

### Another Way to Give

Planned giving allows you to leave a financial gift, a lasting legacy of love, to support Mother Meera’s work. By naming Mother Meera, the Mother Meera Ashram & Japa Yoga Trust, or the Mother Meera Foundation USA as a beneficiary of your estate, you can honor Mother Meera’s mission and ensure continued support for her schools in India and other international programs.

Whether it is a portion of your estate or a specific bequest, your gift can help sustain Mother Meera’s work for generations to come.

If planned giving is something you would like to consider, please contact the Planned Giving Department at `plannedgiving@mothermeerafoundationusa.org` or `(833) 776-3372`.

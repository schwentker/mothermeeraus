This page explains the practical ways people may remain in contact with this work in the United States. Its purpose is simple: to gather ordinary channels for updates, help, and basic support without turning participation into a campaign.

## Social

Social channels can be useful for brief updates, photographs, and reminders when new information is published. They are supplementary, not primary, and important details such as event timing or location should still be confirmed on this site or through a listed contact.

## Volunteer

Volunteer help usually concerns practical matters such as local coordination, welcoming visitors, preparing a space, or helping information reach the right people. This is a way to support the basic conditions around an event, not a separate program or membership track.

## Donations

Donations help cover ordinary needs such as venue costs, travel, printed materials, and site maintenance when those expenses arise. Giving is optional and should remain quiet in tone; it supports the practical work around darshan rather than standing at the center of it.

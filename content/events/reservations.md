This page preserves the reservation guidance that appeared on the former site.

> Progress doesn’t depend on the number of darshans. It depends on their devotion, dedication and sincerity.
> Mother Meera

Starting January 1st 2025, Mother Meera is holding in-person darshan in Schaumburg, Germany. From 7:00 to 7:15 PM Central European time there will be livestreamed meditation with Mother Meera in the Darshan Hall. The livestream will then be switched off and the darshan will begin. From Monday to Friday, and during city trips, Mother Meera will continue to give online and live meditations.

Darshan is free, but you must [make a reservation](https://www.mothermeera.com). For detailed instructions on reserving spaces for yourself and your family, and changing or canceling reservations, please see the [Reservation Guide](https://www.mothermeera.com).

## Reservation FAQs

### Can my child receive darshan from Mother Meera?

Yes. Children are welcome at Mother Meera’s darshan. Children and parents sit together in the front of the darshan hall and are among the first to receive darshan, then leave shortly thereafter. Please make a separate reservation for each child, in addition to your own. All children must be accompanied by a parent or guardian. For their comfort, children should be able to sit quietly for 15–20 minutes.

### I will need assistance in order to receive darshan. Will anyone be available to assist me?

Yes. There are volunteers in the darshan hall who can assist you. Please send an email to `reservationsmmna@gmail.com` letting the organizers know your situation and needs, and they will be happy to make arrangements that will enable you to receive darshan in comfort and safety.

### Will I receive a confirmation of my reservation?

Yes. An email confirmation will be sent after making your reservation. If you did not receive it, please check your spam folder, and add `info@mmdarshan.de`, `MMFUSA@mmdarshan.de`, and `reservationsmmna@gmail.com` to your address book or safelist. Please also check this website regularly after you make a reservation for important updates and information, and check at least one week prior to your darshan for any changes to the schedule.

### Can I go to darshan in more than one location when Mother comes to the US?

Yes. For this tour, guests may attend darshan in more than one location. Note, however, that only one darshan per day is allowed. Please check the tour announcement and this website for changes to this policy.

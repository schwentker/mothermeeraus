## Daily Meditations with Mother Wherever You Are

Beginning Friday March 13th 2020, Mother Meera will hold meditations every day on [YouTube Live](https://www.youtube.com) at 7pm Central European Time (1pm Eastern Daylight Time). If you are not able to connect at the time of the meditation, recordings are available. You can also subscribe to the Mother Meera Live YouTube channel to receive email notifications of upcoming broadcasts and when the meditation is starting.
